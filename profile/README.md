## About Us

Economic AI is a leading **Causal AI** company founded by researchers in Causal Machine Learning, Statistics, and Artificial Intelligence. We make research-based causal inference methods accessible to practitioners and organizations across industries.

Our mission is to move businesses beyond correlation to true **cause-and-effect understanding** — enabling precise treatment effect estimation, counterfactual analysis, and data-driven decision making.
